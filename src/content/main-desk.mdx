# Setting up the Main Desk

Every company needs an HQ. For a solo entrepreneur, your HQ shouldn't be a physical office or even a browser tab. It should be where you already spend your time: **Slack** or **Discord**.

## 🛠 Step 1: Connect the Gateway

The Hermes Agent acts as the bridge between your communication platform and your machine.

### The Architecture
1. **Gateway**: Receives your message.
2. **Orchestrator**: Parses the intent (What do you want?).
3. **Execution**: Runs the required skill.

## 🚀 Simulation: Your First Message

Let's test the connection. In your terminal (or soon, your Slack), type:

```bash
hermes status
```

**Response:**
> **[Hermes]**: Online. 
> - **Active Skills**: WebSearch, FileSystem, Git.
> - **Context**: Solo Entrepreneur Playbook Project.
> - **Status**: Ready for instructions.

## 🎯 Task: Define your "Admin" Channel
Create a private channel in your Slack called `#hq-ops`. This is where you will:
- Receive daily briefings.
- Approve high-priority expenses.
- Monitor your agent's logs.

**Tip**: Don't use your general channel. You want to keep your "CEO Brain" separate from your "Execution Brain".
