00. Introduction to Agent OS

Welcome to Your Agent OS

As a solo entrepreneur, you are the CEO, the CTO, the Marketing Director, and the Customer Support Agent. It's exhausting.

Most people use AI as a "Chatbot"—a tool you talk to when you need something. But to scale as a 1-person company, you need an Operating System.

The Solo Founder's Dilemma

You've tried AI. It's fast, but:

  • You have to explain the context every single time.
  • You have to remind it of your brand voice.
  • You have to manually copy-paste results between tools.

Hermes Agent is not just another chatbot. It is a system that remembers, searches, and executes tasks autonomously.

🗺 Your Transformation Map

Here is how we will build your AI team, step-by-step:

Phase 1: The Core (Foundation)

We start by moving from "Prompting" to "Orchestrating". You'll learn why Hermes is an OS, not just a script.

  • Key Outcome: A running CLI environment where your agent understands your business context.

Phase 2: The Front Door (Architecture)

You shouldn't have to go to the agent. The agent should come to you. We'll set up a "Main Desk" (usually Slack or Discord) where all requests flow in.

  • Key Outcome: Your first "Employee" reachable via your phone.

Phase 3: The Memory Bank (Intelligence)

Agents without memory are just expensive calculators. We'll design a tiered memory system:

  • Long-term: Your core business values and SOPs.
  • Short-term: What happened in the last hour?
  • Shared: What do all your agents know together?

Phase 4: The 24/7 Engine (Tools & Automation)

This is where you go offline. We'll set up cron jobs and Skills so your agents work while you sleep.

  • Key Outcome: A morning briefing bot that summarizes what happened while you were away.
